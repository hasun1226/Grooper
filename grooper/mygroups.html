Long description:

Grooper is a web application which lets the students in University of Toronto Computer Science to find a group for their projects more easily and more efficiently. It will be using a poll-like interface for the users who are looking for groups. Users can give a description about their groups when creating a poll to inform the others what the group is going to be like and the other users who are interested in the group can briefly introduce about themselves when declaring their interest to join the group. The host of the group can select the group mates among the candidates.
